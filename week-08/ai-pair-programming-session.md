# AI Pair Programming Session

> [Outcome engineering](https://o16g.com/): complex outcomes require
> sequenced agent steps, not single prompts. The driver breaks the task
> down and steers. The navigator watches for where the steering goes wrong.
> Both roles are the work.

Two rounds. One driver, one navigator. Then switch.
